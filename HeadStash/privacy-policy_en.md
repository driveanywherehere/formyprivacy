# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-03**

## HeadStash — Privacy Policy

### Data Collection

HeadStash does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

### Data Storage

All data — including worries, people, logs, status history, and settings — is stored **locally on your device** using an embedded SQLite database. No data leaves your device at any time.

### Network Access

HeadStash does **not** make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

### Third-Party Services

HeadStash does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

### User Accounts

HeadStash does **not** require or support user accounts. No registration, login, or authentication is needed.

### Data Deletion

If you delete the HeadStash app from your device, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

### Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the app's support page.

# HeadStash - Support

## About

HeadStash is a local-first iOS worry management app. All data stays on your device — no account, no cloud, no data collection.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your iPhone using an embedded SQLite database. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

**Q: How do I back up my data?**
A: Data is currently stored in the app's sandbox. If you delete the app, data will be lost. We recommend exporting important information before deletion. Future versions will support data export and backup.

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**
A: No. HeadStash works entirely offline. All features are available without network access.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: Chinese, English, and Japanese. You can switch languages anytime within the app.

**Q: How do I mark a worry as solved?**
A: On the worry detail page, tap the "Resolve Now" button, or change the status to "No Action Needed". Status changes are irreversible — you would need to recreate the item.

**Q: What is the Relationship Graph?**
A: The relationship graph visualizes connections between your worries and key people, helping you see who is linked to which worries.

# Privacy Policy

**InPrivate Portfolio Tracker**

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Bixiong Xu ("we", "us", or "our") built InPrivate Portfolio Tracker as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

## Information Collection and Use

InPrivate Portfolio Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. **All your data is stored locally on your device.** We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

### Data Stored Locally

- Trade records, portfolio positions, and cash flow data
- App preferences (language, display currency)

All of the above data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device and never leaves your device.

### Network Requests

The app connects to third-party financial data providers (such as Yahoo Finance) solely to retrieve real-time stock quotes and market data. These requests contain only stock ticker symbols and do not include any personal information.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Since all data resides on your device, we have no access to it.

## Data Security

Your data is stored locally using standard operating system security mechanisms. We recommend keeping your device protected with a password or biometric lock.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since no data is stored on our servers, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

# InPrivate Portfolio Tracker - Support

## About

InPrivate Portfolio Tracker is a local-first investment tracking app for macOS. All data stays on your device — no account, no cloud, no data collection.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your Mac using an embedded database. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

**Q: How do I back up my data?**
A: Your data is stored in the app's local storage directory. You can back up by copying the database file from `~/Library/Application Support/InPrivate Portfolio Tracker/`.

**Q: Which markets are supported?**
A: Any market supported by Yahoo Finance, including US stocks(e.g.`MSFT`), Hong Kong (e.g. `0700.HK`), Shanghai (e.g. `600519.SS`), Shenzhen (e.g. `000858.SZ`), Tokyo, and more.

**Q: What asset types can I track?**
A: Stocks, options, futures, and cash.

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**
A: An internet connection is only needed to fetch real-time market quotes. All other features work offline.
